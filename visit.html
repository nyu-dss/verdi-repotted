Visit the Verdi Archive

Since its inception in 1976, the Verdi Archive has welcomed students, researchers, performers, and opera lovers to its Archive.  Each year we receive a number of visitors from around the world, and offer our support to endeavors ranging from student term papers to doctoral dissertations, books, and  performances by the world's leading opera companies.

Readers are received by appointment only.  Appointments may be made by contacting the AIVS at verdi.institute@nyu.edu.
