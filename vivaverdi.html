VivaVerdi! Newsletter of the American Institute for Verdi Studies

Welcome to welcome to VivaVerdi!, the electronic newsletter produced by the AIVS. Our aim is to provide news and information concerning scholarly activities, recent or forthcoming Verdi performances and recordings, updates on publication plans, calls for papers, and more.

VivaVerdi! is distributed through various lists and musicological websites, and will be
available for download on our homepage.  Please follow the links below to access and download current and past issues of VivaVerdi! (PDF format. Adobe Reader required.)

We very much hope that VivaVerdi! will become an open and interactive forum, and
that our readers will feel encouraged to offer comments and feedback or to
suggest contents for future issues. Please do not hesitate to contact us.
